NISSAN SKYLINE R34 GTR

The Skyline GT-R R34 is inarguably one of the most tuner-friendly

cars around today. It is possible to get the car's engine from 276 hp

all the way to 400 hp with a simple remap, and today, there are some

GT-Rs that prowl the street with 800 or even a 1000 horses

underneath the hood
