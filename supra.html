TOYOTA MK4 SUPRA

The Supra turbo engine is seriously overbuilt. Bone stock it made 320 HP

but that was just a good starting point. The engine itself could handle enough

airflow with the right turbo and fuel system to make 500hp on stock internals.

Upgrading rods and pistons would push that to 800+.

The inline 6 engine was super smooth, sounds wicked, and combine that with RWD

and a suspension that could be made to handle very well, the car is simply fantastic

as it is or even more awesome when modified. Oh, and it’s very easy to work on and

very reliable as well, even when modified. It’s a perfect car for someone who likes

to play with cars.
